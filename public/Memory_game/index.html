Memory Game

You remember that somebody told you how the structure of the army looks like.

But now you are confused and can't focus because of the stress associated with your escape.

You need to collect your thoughts and find right pairs of the army structure.

It is highly important to confirm to our command the size of the unit that has seized our area!

If every pair is allready uncovered. Grab cards with numbers and put them in ascending order.

Take a screenshot if you play by app or photo if you have done it manually and send it to the command!

Password is marked. Use it without a number.
